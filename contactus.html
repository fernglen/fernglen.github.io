Fernglen is owned by the Auckland Council. Education and events are operated through the Fernglen Native Plant Gardens educational committee and trust.

Visitors to Fernglen are most welcome throughout the week during daylight hours. Gardening associations and similar groups may wish to inform Fernglen of their group's visit by emailing at fernglen.nz@gmail.com or telephoning 021 236 5800.

Tree Fern

Fernglen Native Plant Gardens look forward to your visit.

If you would like to receive news from Fernglen or if you have any questions or feedback on our site please email Fernglen.

We will respond to you as quickly as possible. We will never disclose your full name or other personal details.

You can also join our Facebook page and like us.

The Fernglen education room is popular to hire for children's birthday parties, meetings, yoga and more. The room is available for hire. For more please email fernglen.nz@gmail.com
